Custom T-Shirt Terms & Conditions

1. Image Quality & Artwork Requirements

Customers are responsible for providing clear, high-resolution images or designs (PNG or high-quality JPEG recommended). IDK Clothing is not responsible for blurry or pixelated prints resulting from low-quality uploads.

2. Background Removal & Instructions

If your design requires a transparent background, please upload a PNG with the background already removed or explicitly mention in the note section whether background clearing is required. IDK Clothing is not liable for minor variations resulting from automatic background trimming.

3. Fabric & Print Position

Please review your selected cloth/material type, color, size breakdown, and artwork positioning (Center, Left, Right) carefully before submitting. What you select and describe is what will be printed.

4. Final Sale — No Changes After Submission

Once your order is submitted, it immediately enters processing. No changes, modifications, or cancellations can be made after submission. Custom orders are non-refundable unless there is a material defect in the garment.

5. Queries & Support

If you have any doubts about your image resolution, positioning, or custom requests prior to ordering, please reach out to us directly on Instagram: @idkclothing.
